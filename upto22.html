Here you will be able to do what we call "intuitive reading" using the using the 22 Major Arcana of the Rider-White-Smith deck. In this spread, you draw as many cards as you want, until you feel that your question was properly answered.

Please note that this spread can be a bit confusing for begginers, as you could be confused if you get more cards than necessary to interpret the answer.

How you will know when to stop drawing cards? Just by practicing a lot. Usually 3-7 cards are sufficient to give a proper direction. Be aware that here, the order of the cards which are pulled are important and can change the meaning.




(Remember this deck has 22 different cards, so after pulling all 22 you won't be able to draw more cards. To continue reading cards, please reload to have a full deck again.)
