Let's do a three card reading using the 22 Major Arcana of the Rider-White-Smith deck. The three card reading is a very common spread and adaptable, which the meaning of the positions can be suggest some sort of linear path, sequence of events, cause and effect, or a way of getting from point A to point B. Some common meanings for a three card spread are:

  • Past-Present-Future;
  • Body-Mind-Spirit;
  • Situation-Action-Outcome;
  • You-Relationship-Partner;
  • You-Your Path-Your Potential;
  • and many others...

Just think about your question and draw a card by clicking the button bellow. Tha main meanings of the sorted card are described bellow it. Be aware if the card is upright or reversed, as its meanings change.
