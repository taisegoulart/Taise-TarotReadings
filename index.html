Let's do a one-card reading using the 22 Major Arcana of the Rider-White-Smith deck. The one-card reading is used for simple answers, and it is used too for drawing a daily Tarot cards which you give you some advice to your upcoming day.

Just think about your question and draw a card by clicking the button bellow. Tha main meanings of the sorted card are described bellow it. Be aware if the card is upright or reversed, as its meanings change.




(Remember this deck has 22 different cards, so after pulling all 22 you won't be able to draw more cards. To continue reading cards, please reload the page to have a full deck again.)
